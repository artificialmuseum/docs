What?

The Artificial Museum is a web based Augmented Reality Geolocation Application hosted on artificialmuseum.com.

Artworks are positioned in the real world using GPS, allowing artists to freely place their art and visitors to experience the same art anytime.

Who?

The Artificial Museum is a project of TheSystemCollective.

Privacy notice

No spying

We promise to never analyze, track or sell any data about you.

We will also never save any data without asking explicitly.

Local data

All browser features that collect sensitive data (device orientation, 3d-map of your surroundings, camera video streams, geolocation) DO NOT and NEVER WILL transmit this data to our servers.

Session storage

We save some data in your browser (your last gps location).

This information gets deleted as soon as you close the browser tab.
